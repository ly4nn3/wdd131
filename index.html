About Me

I'm Julyanne Lee. I was born in a small town in Malaysia. I'm a straits-born Chinese which is also an ethnic minority here in my home country. I have a cat named KitKat and I always look forward to spending time with her. I love food and music, and I love crafting.

Fun Facts

Straits-born Chinese is known as Peranakan or Baba-Nyonya. Our culture is a unique hybridization of ancient Chinese culture with local cultures of the Nusantara region. Regions with significant populations of Peranakan are Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, and Southern Thailand. There are also Peranakans with Indian ancestry known as Chitty.
